Overview

Digital ordering without the hardware headache.

A SaaS platform that turns any venue's tables into smart ordering stations — using QR codes that guests scan with their own smartphones, with no app download, no dedicated hardware, and no complex installation.

Traditional POS and ordering systems cost €5,000–€30,000+ in hardware alone — tablet terminals, card readers, kitchen display units, and cabling. For independent restaurants, cafés, and retail venues, that capital outlay is a barrier to digital ordering adoption. Our QR Ordering SaaS eliminates the hardware entirely.

Guests scan a QR code placed on the table — printed on a card, engraved on a stand, or stuck to the surface — and the full menu appears in their browser. No app download required. They browse, select, customise, and pay — all from their own phone. The order appears instantly on the kitchen display and the venue admin dashboard.

The platform is managed entirely through a web-based admin dashboard — venue owners can update menus, change prices, add daily specials, and manage tables in real time without technical knowledge. Integrated payment processing via Stripe handles Apple Pay, Google Pay, and card — with funds settled directly to the venue's bank account.

How It Works

From QR scan to kitchen

The complete guest ordering journey — from QR code scan to kitchen display notification, in under 90 seconds.

01
Guest Scans QR Code
Guest picks up phone camera and scans the QR code on the table. Opens instantly in mobile browser — no app download, no login required. Menu displayed in 1–2 seconds.
02
Browses & Selects
Full menu displayed with categories, item descriptions, photos, allergen information, and pricing. Guest adds items to cart, selects quantities, and adds special instructions.
03
Pays at Table
Guest pays via Apple Pay, Google Pay, card, or selects "pay at counter." Payment processed securely via Stripe — no card terminal required. Confirmation displayed instantly.
04
Kitchen Gets the Order
Order appears instantly on the kitchen display screen and venue admin dashboard — with table number, items, quantities, and any special instructions. No paper, no shouting across the room.
